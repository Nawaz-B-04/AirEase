Dos:
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1- Do research on the airport layout and services beforehand to make the most of your time there.
=====================================================================================================================
 2- Do be courteous and patient when dealing with airport personnel and other passengers.
=====================================================================================================================
 3- Do make sure your carry-on luggage is within size and weight limits to avoid additional fees.
=====================================================================================================================

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Don'ts:
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1- Don't leave your luggage unattended, as it may be confiscated or cause security concerns.
=====================================================================================================================
 2- Don't engage in illegal or disruptive behavior, such as smoking in non-smoking areas or causing a disturbance.
=====================================================================================================================
 3- Don't attempt to bring prohibited items through security checkpoints, as this may result in fines, arrest.
=====================================================================================================================
